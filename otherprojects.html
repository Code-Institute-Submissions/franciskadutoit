Other Projects

Over the last few years I have been working on a variety of projects either part of my previous roles or on the side. Here you will find some of those projects.

Project Synergy at Sanlam

I was part of a team that researched, designed and presented a project for a central portal where all departments within Sanlam can add their projects, to ensure there is no duplication, enable participation, ensure compliance and promote cost effectiveness.

Fincede Business Website

I designed my first site for a business on Wordpress for Fincede Finacial Services. This project help me understand the basics of website layouts and design.

Sanlam Conversations with Clients Project

A colleague and I started a webinar series about topical issues in the financial industry and we reached over a 1000 people. I also designed the marketing material for the series.

Leasing Hub at Quadient

I designed and developed a sharepoint site as a central hub for information and learning material for account managers. I also wrote a few articles for the site and designed the marketing material.
